\texte{
On consid\`ere l'application $u$ suivante~:
$$
 u~:\begin{array}{ccc}
     \R_{n}[X] & \rightarrow & \R_{n}[X] \\
     P         &\mapsto & P'
   \end{array}
$$
Calculer ${}^{t}u(\alpha)$ lorsque~:
$$
\alpha~: P\mapsto P(0) \qquad \qquad \qquad \alpha~: P\mapsto \int_{0}^{1}P(t)dt
$$
}
